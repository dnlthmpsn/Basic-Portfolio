About Me

Hey, My name is Daniel, and that handsome black dog is Merlin. He's my moms dog; a christmas present from my siblings and me. It was sort of to make up for the skydiving accident. I took my mom skydiving and she really enjoyed it, but she broke her ankle on the landing. So we bought her a puppy for Christmas.

We also gave her Merlin because we are dog people and our house had been dogless for a long time. We named him Merlin because.. he's magic. My experience in University, being a self starter, and working for other entrepreneurs have melded together to ultimately convince me that it's time to take the plunge; so here I am!
